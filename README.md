[cite_start]**Project Vanthic** is a sci-fi exploration FPS set on the alien planet KHALDRYX-9[cite: 3, 5, 13]. [cite_start]As a Tuner, the player must explore an abandoned research station, master elemental energies, and uncover the secrets of the mysterious Vanth Core[cite: 13, 15].

## 🎮 Game Overview

[cite_start]The gameplay focuses on a classic 3D Metroidvania loop: exploring interconnected environments, facing challenging enemies, and acquiring new elemental abilities to unlock previously inaccessible areas[cite: 30, 31, 35].

### Core Pillars
* [cite_start]**Dynamic Combat:** Fast and responsive engagement with high mobility[cite: 7, 39].
* [cite_start]**Smart Exploration:** Interconnected world with a focus on backtracking and shortcuts[cite: 8, 81].
* [cite_start]**Elemental Progression:** Advancement is driven by mastery over different types of energy[cite: 9, 48].
* [cite_start]**Environmental Storytelling:** The narrative is delivered through the environment, audio logs, and terminals[cite: 10, 25, 26].

## 🗺️ Level Design (Interconnected World)

The game takes place in distinct sectors that converge at a central HUB:
1. [cite_start]**Research Ring (HUB):** The player's main connection and orientation point[cite: 135, 137, 139].
2. [cite_start]**Electrical Sector:** Industrial complexes with exposed circuitry[cite: 153, 159].
3. [cite_start]**Cryogenic Sector:** Low-visibility environments and fragile structures[cite: 176, 181, 182].
4. [cite_start]**Magmatic Fissures:** High-pressure zones and mobile platforms over lava[cite: 197, 201, 208].
5. [cite_start]**Vanth Core:** The technological and alien climax where all skills are tested[cite: 222, 226, 237].
